    • HIGHLIGHTS

    For me, Going Seventeen's EGO is one of the most unique concepts they featured. Back in 2020, they released a horror special called "The Tag" where they visited a "haunted house" escape room. In 2021, EGO was released with a complete, film-accented storyline.

    EGO is filled with suspenseful, mhumorour, and mysterious scenes that I particularly liked. In this page, I will be highlighting some of my personal favorite scenes.

    ⚠ (WARNING! This page may contain spoilers.) ⚠

    1. Hoshi Gets Caught

    This was the very first jumpscare-type situation in this show. When Hoshi watched his partner getting taken away, he stood for a moment before he decided to go inside the locker for some unknown reason. Things took a turn when he stepped out and made eye contact with the doctor who suddenly stood in front of the door.

    3. S.Coups and Joshua Gets Chased

    Followed by a sudden slam of the door, Scoups sprang up the stairs before Joshua. Right behind them, the doctor strided slowly which gave a pang of eeriness.

    5. Jeonghan and DK Frees the Hostage

    This was a very funny scene which deserved two different highlights from me. Jeonghan and DK, who were both doubtful of freeing the hostage calling out to them, started asking the man what to do because they were too scared to do anything else. Finally, DK freed the man from his seat.

    6. Sacrificing the Hostage

    After saving the hostage, Jeonghan and DK told the man in two separate occasions to walk ahead of them because "they saved him so he had to be grateful." They even made him open a locker, which was talked about in the clue they got, so whatever was inside would take the hostage instead of them.

    7. Clones' Memories

    8. A Call to the Doctor

    This was the moment when chills ran down my spine. The two were figuring out which key could be used at the exit door but the sudden screech of their previous friend jumped them to freeze. They stared at him in confusion for a while, but let out a disappointed sigh and questions why he had done it. To make things worse, the "hostage" even gracefully opened the door for the doctor to come in and take them.
